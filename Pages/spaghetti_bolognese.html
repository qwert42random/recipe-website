Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Olive Oil
  • Onion
  • Oregano
  • Salt
  • Garlic Paste

Instructions:

  1. Bring large post of lightly salted water to a rolling boil. Cook spaghetti in the boiling water for about 12 minutes until through yet first to the bite. Then drain.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add and stir onion for 8 to 10 minutes with garlic paste.
  3. Add spaghetti sauce.
  4. Mix together with spaghetti and serve.
