Beef Stew

Ingredients:

  • Beef
  • Flour
  • Oil
  • Garlic
  • Wine
  • Vegetables
  • Beef Stock
  • Tomato Puree
  • Pepper & Salt

Instructions:

  1. Season beef with pepper and salt.
  2. Add one spoon of flour and stir.
  3. Add oil, 2 cloves of garlic and simmer in pot.
  4. Add beef to pot and stir.
  5. Add wine.
  6. Chop vegetables and add to pot.
  7. Add beef stock.
  8. Add tomato puree.
  9. leave in oven for 2 hours at 120 degrees.
